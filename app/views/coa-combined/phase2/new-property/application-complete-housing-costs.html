What happens next

We are doing some final checks on your application.

We will contact you by letter if we can award your Pension Credit. If we need more information, we will contact you by phone if you have provided a contact number, or letter if you have only provided an address.

What you need to do next

Send us information about your service charges and ground rent

Send us a copy of the most recent ground rent invoice and the most recent service charge invoice, with an itemised breakdown of charges.

Post any requested documents to:

Northern Ireland Pension Centre
Mail Opening Unit
PO Box 42
Limavady
BT49 4AW

The Pension Service 4
Mail Handling Site A
Wolverhampton
WV98 1AG

Include (your/the applicant's) name and National Insurance number with anything you send. Any original documents will be returned to the address you have asked us to send any letters to.

Other help you could get

There is other help that you could get if you:

  • have a disability
  • care for someone for 35 hours or more a week
  • need help with your living costs such as paying rent or Council Tax
  • need help heating your home

To check what other help you could get, use one of these benefits calculators.
