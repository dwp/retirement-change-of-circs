Did you have any housing costs/service charges where you previously lived?

Housing costs are anything that isn't currently covered by housing benefit. This can include

  • warden services
  • caretaker services like gardening, maintenance and cleaning of communal areas
  • use of a laundry room
  • use of communal areas like a lounge or TV area
