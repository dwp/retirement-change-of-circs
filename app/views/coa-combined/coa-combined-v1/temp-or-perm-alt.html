Do you plan to move out before 28 February 2026?

If you plan to move out in less than 6 months, you should update the address we send your letters to.

If you are planning to stay at your new property for over 6 months, this is deemed a permanent address which could affect how much Pension Credit you're entitled to.

You will need to inform Pension Credit of this change once you have changed your address with State Pension.
