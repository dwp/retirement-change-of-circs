We need more information from you

To make sure we are paying you the correct amount of Pension Credit, we need more information from you.

We will contact you by phone or we will send you forms to fill out.

If your circumstances change

You need to tell us if you, or anyone who lives with you:

  • goes into hospital or moves into a care home
  • has a change to their income, for example they stop working, or you start getting another pension or social security benefit
  • leaves England, Scotland and Wales for more than 4 weeks
  • goes to prison or are held in legal custody
  • changes their name
  • has their immigration status changed

We also need to know if:

  • you get married or form a civil partnership
  • you get divorced or your civil partnership is dissolved
  • anyone moves in or out of your home
  • you become a carer for someone
  • someone starts caring for you
  • someone you live with dies
  • you change address
  • you want to change your contact details
  • you want us to pay you into a different bank account
  • there is a change to the amount you pay in service charges or ground rent
  • your money and investments go above £10,000

We may stop or reduce your payments, and you may have to pay back money, if you:

  • do not report a change straight away
  • give wrong information
  • are paid too much

Some changes will increase the amount of Pension Credit you could get.

Call us if you are not sure if you need to report a change.

If you live in England, Scotland or Wales:

  • call us on 0800 731 0469

  • go to GOV.UK to find other ways to report changes
