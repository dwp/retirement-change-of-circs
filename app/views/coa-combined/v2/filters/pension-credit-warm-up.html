Changing your address when you get State Pension and Pension Credit

We need to ask you some questions to update your State Pension address and to make sure you get the right amount of Pension Credit. We will ask about:

  • your new address
  • service charges
  • how much money you have in bank and savings accounts
  • your contact details

If you have a partner, we will ask about:

  • your partner's date of birth and National Insurance number
  • how much money you and your partner have in bank and savings accounts

Before you continue

You will need to answer all the questions in one go. You cannot save your answers and return later.

For security, we will sign you out after 30 minutes of inactivity.

Would you like to update your address for State Pension and Pension Credit?

We will need more information from you to check if there will be a change to your Pension Credit entitlement.
