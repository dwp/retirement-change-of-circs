Service charges and ground rent

Service charges

Service charges can include:

  • warden and caretaker services like gardening, maintenance and cleaning of communal areas
  • insurance charges for the building (not for personal possessions)
  • use of a lift or laundry room
  • use of communal areas like a lounge or tv area
  • removal of refuse

Ground rent

You might pay ground rent if your home is a leasehold property.
