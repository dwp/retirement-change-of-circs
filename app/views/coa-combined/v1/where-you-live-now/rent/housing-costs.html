Do you pay housing costs or ground rent?

Housing costs can include:

  • warden and caretaker services
  • caretaker services (like gardening, maintenance and cleaning of communal areas)
  • Insurance charges - compulsory for structure of building, not for personal posessions
  • use of a lift or laundry room
  • use of a communcal areas like a lounge or tv area
  • removal of refuse

You might pay ground rent if your home is a leasehold property.
