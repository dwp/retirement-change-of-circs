One login authentication

  • Lucid board with designs
  • JIRA ticket for UCD

Informing citizens of the new GOV.UK One Login that's being rolled out across government.

It's important to let citizens know that even though they may have registered to use our service before, they will need to create a new .GOV.UK One Login to continue using our service.

There is a 'sign in or create' link on the start page which will take citizens into the One Login service where they can either sign in or create an account

Citizens can choose to sign in if they already have a GOV.UK One Login however they may not be able to use the service yet if their record is in a legacy system.

Citizens can see what they will likely be able to do if they click on the start button which will take them through 2 questions to work out if they can use this service or not.
