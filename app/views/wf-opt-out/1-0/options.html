Using Manage your State Pension

Manage your State Pension is the quickest way to be opted in or out of getting the Winter Fuel Payment. You can only use Manage your State Pension if you are getting State Pension.

If you are not getting State Pension or were born after 1955

You may not be able to use the Manage your State Pension service to opt in or out of the Winter Fuel Payment. You should use the online form or contact the Winter Fuel Payment Centre.
