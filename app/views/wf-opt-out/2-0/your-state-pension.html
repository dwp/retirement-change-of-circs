    Opt out of getting the Winter Fuel Payment

    If you are eligible for the Winter Fuel Payment but do not want to get it you can ask to be opted out.

    Why you might want to opt out

    If your total income is over £35,000, HMRC will take your Winter Fuel Payment back. Your partner’s income does not count towards your total.

    Opting back in and reporting other changes

    Contact the Winter Fuel Payment Centre (opens in new tab) if you want to:

    • opt back in to getting the Winter Fuel Payment
    • need to report a change of circumstances
    • need to change your address or personal details
