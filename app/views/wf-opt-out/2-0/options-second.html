Signing in to Manage your State Pension

To sign in you will need a GOV.UK One Login. If you do not have a GOV.UK One Login you'll need to create one.

Your GOV.UK One Login can be used to access other Government services. In the future, you’ll be able to use it to access all services on GOV.UK.

Find out more about GOV.UK One Login.

Prove your identity

To help keep your information safe, you may be asked to prove your identity.

You'll need:

  • a valid photo ID, such as a passport or driving licence

Or, if you haven't got a photo ID, you can prove your identity with:

  • your bank or building society account details
  • your National Insurance number
