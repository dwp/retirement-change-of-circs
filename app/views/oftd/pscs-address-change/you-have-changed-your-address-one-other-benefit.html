What you need to do next

You must report your change of address for any other benefits you get or have applied for. You must contact the Attendance Allowance helpline (opens in new tab).

If you do not report your change of address your payments may stop.

What happens next

We will send you an email when we have changed your address for State Pension. This will be within the next 8 days.

Contacting us

You may need to contact us:

  • if you need to get in touch urgently
  • to change the address we send letters to
  • to find out what other changes you must report, and how to tell us about them

You can contact:

  • the Pension Service (opens in new tab) if you live in England, Scotland or Wales
  • the Northern Ireland Pension Centre (opens in new tab) if you live in Northern Ireland
