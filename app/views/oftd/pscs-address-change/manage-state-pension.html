Manage your State Pension

What you can do

You can use this service to:

  • report that you have moved to a new address
  • change your bank details
  • check when you'll get your next State Pension payment

This is a new service. You'll be able to report other changes online in the future.

Changing your payment account details

It may take us up to 8 days to update your details.

If you do not have access to your old account, and your next payment is due within the next 8 days, you should report your change by phone.

Before you start

You'll need to register if you have not used this service before.

To register you'll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • an email address
  • a mobile phone number

You'll also need to answer some security questions to confirm your identity. This helps protect you from fraud.

If you have already registered

You'll need the email address, mobile phone and password you used to register.

Start now

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

If you did not claim your State Pension online, or you need to tell us about other changes

If you cannot report your changes online, contact the Pension Service by phone or post.

If you live in Northern Ireland, contact the Northern Ireland Pension Centre by phone or post.
