Trader Goods Profile

As a UK Internal Market Scheme (UKIMS) member, we’ve enrolled you to Trader Goods Profile (TGP).

TGP will help you when moving goods from Great Britain to Northern Ireland.

TGP can do this by:

  • letting you manage your goods records
  • giving intermediaries access to your goods records to complete the Simplified Process for Internal Market Movements (SPIMM)
  • letting you know if you can move your goods with the Internal Market Movement Information (IMMI)
  • letting you ask HMRC for an opinion if you’re using the correct goods information

What you can do

Search for ‘Manage your Trader Goods Profile’ on a browser to find the service. You’ll need the Government Gateway user ID and password you registered for the Customs Declaration Service (CDS).

Unsubscribe
If you’re unsure an email is from HMRC:
  • Do not reply to it or click on any links
  • Report the suspicious email to HMRC - to find out how, go to GOV.UK and search for 'Avoid and report internet scams and phishing'
