Manage your State Pension

Changes you can report online

You can use this service to tell us you have changed your address.

You cannot tell us about a move to prison using this service.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Other changes you can make online

If you were born after 1955 you may be able to change:

  • how often we pay your State Pension
  • when you'll get your next State Pension payment
  • where we send your letters
  • your phone number
  • your email address

Tell us about changes by phone or post

If you live in England, Scotland or Wales contact the Pension Service by phone or post.

If you live in Northern Ireland contact the Northern Ireland Pension Centre by phone or post.
