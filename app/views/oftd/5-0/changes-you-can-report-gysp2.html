Changes you can report online

You can use this service to change the account we pay your State Pension into.

It is likely you can also use this service to change:

  • the account we pay State Pension into
  • your phone number
  • your email address
  • how often we pay your State Pension
  • the address where you live
  • when you'll get your next State Pension payment
