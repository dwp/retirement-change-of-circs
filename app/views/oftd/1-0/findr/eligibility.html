Manage your State Pension

Use this service to:

  • change your bank details
  • change your phone number
  • change your email address
  • change how often we pay your State Pension
  • change the address where you live
  • check when you'll get your next State Pension payment

This is a new service. You'll be able to report other changes online in the future.

If you need to tell us about something else

If you live in England, Scotland or Wales contact the Pension Service by phone or post.

If you live in Northern Ireland, contact the Northern Ireland Pension Centre by phone or post.

Check you can use this service

If you've used this service before, you can sign in.

You'll need the email address, mobile phone and password you used to register.

If you're using this service for the first time, you'll need to register.

We will check that you can register by asking your:

  • date of birth
  • full name
  • postcode

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
