Your request to change your bank details for your State Pension

We have received your request to change your bank details for State Pension.

It may take us up to 8 days to update your bank details. We will send you another email when this is done.

If your next payment is due within 8 days, your next payment may:

  • be delayed
  • be paid into your old bank account

If you need to tell us about any other changes

You can call us on 0800 731 0469 if this will cause any problems.

To report any other changes, like a change of address, contact the Pension Service by phone or by post. You can find their contact details at www.gov.uk/contact-pension-service/report-changes
