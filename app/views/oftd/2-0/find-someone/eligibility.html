Manage your State Pension

You must be getting your State Pension to use this service.

You can use this service to view or change:

  • your bank details
  • your phone number
  • your email address
  • how often we pay your State Pension
  • the address where you live
  • when you'll get your next State Pension payment

If you have used this service before

If you've used this service before, you can sign in.

You'll need the email address, mobile phone and password you used to register.

If you have not used this service before

This service is new and can only be used by some people.

Before you register we will check whether you are likely to be able to use the service.

If you need to contact us another way or tell us about other changes

If you live in England, Scotland or Wales contact the Pension Service by phone or post.

If you live in Northern Ireland, contact the Northern Ireland Pension Centre by phone or post.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
